Opus One Design Build incorporates environmentally friendly practices to help reduce our footprint during Construction, and to improve the quality of life for property occupants and surrounding neighbors.


We use materials such as low-emitting adhesives and paints to promote healthier indoor environments, as often as possible. We try to reuse and recycle materials as much as possible without compromising our quality standards and our clients’ design requirements.

We focus on green and sustainable building practices with extensive construction experience spanning LEED techniques. We work closely with our partners to ensure the consideration and application of sustainable materials and energy efficient systems are deployed whenever feasible. Additionally, we focus on waste minimization and recycling/reclamation of demolition and renovation materials to ensure our environmental footprint is minimal.
