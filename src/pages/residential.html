Getting Your Residential Construction Project Started

We encourage those considering a project in the next 3-12 months to contact us so that we may determine, via a brief telephone conversation with one of our Project Managers, whether an in-person visit makes sense. If it does, an onsite consultation will be scheduled with one of the members of our team. Our consultations typically last approximately one hour and require the property owner(s) to be present, along with any additional decision makers who will be involved with the project. The meetings are friendly, informal and interactive experiences. Our Project Manager will do a walk-through of the property with you, and will then sit with you to discuss options, preferences, timing, and to answer any questions you may have.
