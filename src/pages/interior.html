An Award-winning Interior Design Team

Architecture And Interior Design – The Opus design team is comprised of an eclectic group of Architects, Interior Designers, Space Planners and AutoCAD artists, all of whom seek to design from a perspective that is culturally, financially and historically responsible.


Some of the best work from the Opus One Design Build team has been published and featured in local and national magazines and editorials. Our sensitivity to existing construction has been recognized by The American Institute of Architects (AIA). Recognized as innovators in the Architecture And Interior Design space, we are often called upon by other Construction, Engineering, Project Management and Design firms to collaborate on their Client projects.

Whether we are your Design advisors as part of a larger project, or the drivers behind stand-alone service, a dedicated Project Manager will call upon our in-house design experts as needed in order to walk you through each phase of your project. Your Project Manager will be with you from inception to completion and will be your Central Point of Contact (CPOC) so that you never have to wonder about the progress or status of your project. In addition, all of our projects have owner-oversight.

Get Your Architecture or Interior Design Project Started

We encourage those considering a project in the next 3-12 months to contact us so that we may determine, via a brief telephone conversation with one of our Project Managers, whether an in-person visit makes sense. If it does, an onsite consultation will be scheduled with one of our Project Estimators. Our consultations typically last approximately one hour and require the property owner(s) to be present, along with any additional decision makers who will be involved with the project.

The consultations are friendly, informal and interactive experiences. Our Project Manager will do a walk-through of the property with you, and will then sit with you to discuss options, preferences, timing, and to answer any questions you may have.
